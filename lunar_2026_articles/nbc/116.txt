After Brad Ledwith’s sons learned about lacrosse, he quickly found out there was no team or league for them to play in. The father became determined to create a youth program in his hometown of Morgan Hill, California. Brad and his son, Ben, join NBC News Daily to talk about his documentary, “The Might Oaks,” that follows a community that banded together to bring the sport, their children, and love closer to home.
