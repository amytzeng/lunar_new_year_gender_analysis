The FBI said that the ransom note they are investigating in relation to the disappearance of Nancy Guthrie, the mother of "TODAY" co-host Savannah Guthrie, gave a deadline of 5 p.m. on February 5. Authorities, as well as the family of the 84 year old, emphasized that they want to communicate with whomever distributed the ransom note to local and national news outlets.
